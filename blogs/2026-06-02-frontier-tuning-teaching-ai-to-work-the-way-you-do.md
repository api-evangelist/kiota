Microsoft introduced Frontier Tuning, a reinforcement learning approach that enables organizations to customize AI agents using their own business data, processes, and workflows while maintaining compliance boundaries. The system operates through three components: a managed Reinforcement Learning Environment for training and inference, integration of company-specific domain knowledge, and production of tuned models within organizational compliance controls. Currently in private preview through Forward Deployed Engineers, with planned rollout to Microsoft Copilot Studio and Microsoft Foundry, early partners including EY, Pearson, and Bristol Myers Squibb have already demonstrated significant results.
