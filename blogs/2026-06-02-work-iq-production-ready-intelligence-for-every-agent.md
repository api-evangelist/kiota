Microsoft announced that Work IQ API endpoints will become generally available on June 16, 2026, offering agents access to organizational data across Microsoft 365 and external systems with built-in governance. The platform provides a unified intelligence layer combining chat, context, tools, and workspaces to enable agent-first workflows that operate independently of Microsoft 365 Copilot licensing on a consumption basis. Early adopters including SLB, HP, and Miro are already integrating Work IQ to enhance enterprise intelligence capabilities across analysis, device experiences, and collaborative tools.
